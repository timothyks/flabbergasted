Shapeoff 1: A Rocky Start

Aired: June 13, 2024

Estimated Runtime: 17 minutes

The Shapeoff series kicks off with a literal bang as a seemingly ordinary school day is shattered by Triangle and Hexagon blowing up the building. The explosion flings the soon-to-be contestants all the way to Flabbergasted Peaks, where they encounter the mysterious host. In this chaotic first episode, the contestants must race down the mountain in a desperate bid for immunity.

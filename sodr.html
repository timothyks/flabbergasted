Shapeoff Danganronpa is an official spin-off of the Shapeoff series, inspired by the Danganronpa franchise. In this multiple-chapter novel, ten shapes find themselves trapped in a deadly game where they must kill each other to leave.

This story explores darker themes and character dynamics, diverging from the main Shapeoff storyline. It offers a fresh perspective with new characters, new relationships and new motives. Expect mystery, suspense, and unexpected twists as the shapes navigate their dire situation.

Battle For Mom Simulator

A joke object show about fighting for the ultimate video game.

BFMS is a satirical "animated" object show, currently with two seasons. There's not much in the world of Battle For Mom Simulator other than poorly drawn shapes and depressing voice acting.
... or is there?

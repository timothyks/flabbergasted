The camera moves up, overlooking the sunset. However, the screen suddenly goes black.

FADE IN:

INT. RANDOM CAVE - SUNDOWN

Host teleports into this cave with a neutral expression. He walks to a spot where there’s a small bed and a table, with a picture of what seems to be a planet that is not the one the Shapelands are located in.

Host picks up the framed picture, looking at it with a determined expression.

HOST

I’ll show you guys soon enough… Show you guys that I’m actually worth… something.

A tear forms on Host’s eye. He closes his eyes while still holding the picture of the planet (why does he get so emotional to a random planet???).

FADE OUT:

EXT. HARASSMENT HILLS - SUNDOWN

Triangle and Nonagon are sitting next to each other, discussing their vote for tonight.

TRIANGLE

I know you just got here, but do you have an idea on who you’re going to vote for?

NONAGON

Hmm… Well, to me, Hexagon was the one leading the charge in rescuing you. So I think she deserves to stay.

TRIANGLE

Alright, *sigh* I’m not sure on who to vote… so.. Oval I guess?

NONAGON

You don’t think any of them deserve to go, huh?

TRIANGLE

Yep.

Pentagon walks up to Oval, sitting on the grass.

OVAL

Oh, hey, Pentagon! What’s up?

PENTAGON

Eh, not much. Today was SOMETHING, especially for me. Anyways, I just wanted to say that I’m not voting for you tonight.

OVAL

Oh thanks, appreciate it. … Anyways, why didn’t you get teleported back?

PENTAGON

Probably because the Host didn’t know where I was.

OVAL

What? I thought you were paired up with Octagon.

PENTAGON

Yeah I was, but when we were on our journey, I fell into a gigantic ravine! Wanna hear the story of how I got out?

OVAL

Yeah, sure!

PENTAGON

Alright, so, as I fell, I-

Host teleports back, cutting Pentagon off. (gosh darn it, Host!!!).

HOST

I think that was plenty of time for discussion! Everyone, you know what to do!

OVAL

Dude, this is like, the second time you’ve interrupted a conversation between me and Pentagon.

HOST

Yeah so?

OVAL

Um, it’s kinda rude…?

HOST

Uhhhhhhhh Anyways, you guys know what to do! Line up at the voting booth and cast your vote!

INT. VOTING BOOTH - NIGHT

Below are the individual “confessionals” in the voting booth as the contestants cast their vote, one by one.

TRIANGLE

Well, it’s a tough vote, but.. Sorry, Oval.

RECTANGLE

Hexagon’s the one that got us out of that school, so I’m voting Oval!

SQUARE

Hexagon’s too chaotic for me, so she has to go. Sorry Hexagon.

PENTAGON

This time I’m not gonna make the mistake of voting Oval!

TRAPEZOID

(silence)

OVAL

… As much as I hate Hexagon’s chaoticness… I think that she… deserves to stay. She’s the one that led us to McCircle’s and the main driving force of our team today. … I… won’t mind if I get booted tonight… I just want one of my other friends to get far… Unlike my… Nevermind. I’ve been rambling.

EXT. VOTING AREA - NIGHT

Scene changes to outside of the voting booth, where Oval steps out, looking depressed. The rest of the contestants are sitting down, waiting for Host to start reading the votes.

HOST

Aaaand… That should be everyone! If y’all are ready, let’s begin!

Host extends his arm to unrealistic proportions and gets the votes from inside the voting booth.

As Host reads them, he turns the slips of paper over, showing them to the contestants, as usual.

HOST

Okay, first vote… Hexagon.

HOST

Second vote, Hexagon. Hexagon is now at two votes, Oval at zero.

Hexagon starts to get worried…

HOST

Third vote, Oval.

Oval looks contempt with himself.

HOST

Fourth vote, Oval. Hexagon and Oval are both tied with two votes. Five votes left.

Triangle and Nonagon look at each other.

HOST

Fifth vote, Oval.

HOST

Sixth vote, Hexagon. Oval and Hexagon are tied again. Three votes left.

Oval starts to look a bit worried. Guess he didn’t expect that he would ACTUALLY go tonight.

HOST

Seventh vote, Hexagon. If Hexagon gets one more vote, she is guaranteed to be eliminated.

Hexagon is anxiously shaking. Oval is still a bit worried.

HOST

Eighth vote, Oval. Hexagon and Oval are tied, AGAIN, with four votes each! This last vote will determine who gets eliminated tonight!

HOST

And the third one NOT getting to control the Shapelands is…

Hexagon is now VIOLENTLY shaking.

HOST

Oval. I’m sorry, but you have been officially eliminated. You’ve played a great game.

OVAL

What…? No…

PENTAGON

No!!! Oval!

Oval sighs.

OVAL

I knew I wasn’t going to get that far. Truthfully, I just wanted YOU to get far, Pentagon.

PENTAGON

What?!

OVAL

I’ve never been the… competitive type, y’know? But it seems that you’re enjoying this game a lot, so I wish you luck going forward.

PENTAGON

Thanks, Oval! I’m sorry you had to go so early.

OVAL

You don’t have to be.

Oval looks up at everyone else.

OVAL

Goodbye, everyone. It was fun playing this game with you all.

TRIANGLE

Bye Oval…

NONAGON

Yeah, farewell.

OCTAGON

Maybe next time don’t violently tackle me down into a pit? Heh.

OVAL

Alright, Host guy. I’m ready to go.

HOST

If you say so. Goodbye Oval!

CLAP

Oval dramatically falls to the floor.

PENTAGON

Man… I never got to tell Oval what I did in that ravine.

OCTAGON

Oh yeah, what DID happen? How’d you survive?

PENTAGON

If you really wanna know, then I guess I’ll tell you the story. So basically, as I was falling I noticed that-

As Pentagon talks, his voice gets quieter as the camera zooms out, showing the tall tower ONCE AGAIN, with the unknown shape looking at the destruction Hexagon caused.

The elevator dings and opens, and Sphere steps out of it.

SPHERE

I’m back.

Have you seen what they did?

What?

UNKNOWN

Look. They’re constantly destroying establishments left and right.

Sphere looks out, seeing the smoke from where Papa Polygon’s and McCircle’s used to be.

SPHERE

I see. What should we do?

Long dramatic pause ensues.

UNKNOWN

We’re going to have to shut them down.

Sudden black screen.

Shapeoff is a competition webseries told in screenplay format, with an enigmatic host and a cast of colorful contestants.

What begins as a game slowly reveals something watching from the outside: something that doesn't want anyone to win.

Born in 2022 from ideas I had back in 2018, Shapeoff began as a YouTube animated series. It ran for 3 episodes before being cancelled due to burnout. Later, episodes 4â€“6 were revived as slideshow presentations by a friend, leading to a final animated episode 7.

Today, Shapeoff lives on in screenplay format. With 6 episodes written (some over 40 minutes in length!), the series continues as a surreal blend of satire, mystery, and shape-based drama.

Writer’s notes:

  • Starting in Shapeoff 5, episodes actually have themes to them like any good story. One of Shapeoff 5’s main themes is “some things that seem too late may not be.” A theme that I think you all have picked up in this episode is about “finding your voice.” I hope that from now on you all will see the episodes in a different light, as this isn’t just a funny game show where contestants dump soup in their hands anymore.

  • Also, I must address the abrupt ending. I believe that the short ending is a bridge leading to the next episode, if that makes sense. I want to save long endings for stories that, well, end. When I was watching one of the WOIM episodes, Rhombagon had a good point, and that was you don’t just put scenes in to put scenes in. Good stories don’t include filler scenes. That’s about it.
